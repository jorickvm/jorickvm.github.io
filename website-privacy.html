AtlasDays Website Waitlist Privacy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

This page applies to the AtlasDays website waitlist form only. It does not describe the in-app privacy model.

TL;DR

If you choose to join the waitlist on the website, we collect the email address you submit so we can send AtlasDays launch updates.

The form is processed through Formspree.

You can contact us to ask for your waitlist email to be removed.

1. What We Collect

If you submit the optional waitlist form on the website, we collect the email address you provide.

2. Why We Collect It

We use that email address to send AtlasDays launch updates and occasional product news related to the launch period.

3. Form Processing

The waitlist form is processed through Formspree, which acts as a form-processing service for the website.

4. Scope

  • Submitting the waitlist form is optional.
  • The website waitlist is separate from the app itself.
  • We do not ask for more information than your email address on the waitlist form.
  • We do not sell waitlist data.

5. Removal Requests

If you want your waitlist email removed, contact atlasdays [at] pm.me.

6. Changes to This Page

If the AtlasDays website waitlist handling changes, this page will be updated and the date above will change.
