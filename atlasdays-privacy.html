AtlasDays Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

AtlasDays is a trip-tracking app that helps you log the countries you have visited and understand your travel history. Your privacy is important. This policy explains exactly what data the app collects, where it is stored, and what it is used for.

1. Data You Enter

All trip data you create in AtlasDays — including country visits, dates, and notes — is stored exclusively on your device using SwiftData. This data is never transmitted to any server or third party.

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, SwiftData may sync your data across your own devices via your personal iCloud account. This sync happens between your devices only and is governed by Apple's iCloud privacy policy.

2. Location Data

AtlasDays optionally requests "Always On" location access to detect when you enter a new country and suggest adding a trip automatically. This feature is entirely opt-in.

  • Location is monitored using iOS's significant location change service, which uses low-power cell-tower and Wi-Fi signals rather than continuous GPS.
  • Your location is used only to reverse-geocode a country code on-device using Apple's MapKit framework. The resulting country code (e.g. "FR") is stored locally on your device.
  • Your precise location coordinates are never stored, logged, or transmitted outside your device.
  • You can revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → AtlasDays.

3. Notifications

AtlasDays may send local notifications to prompt you to review a trip when a new country is detected. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any external servers or push notification services.

4. CSV Export & Import

AtlasDays allows you to export your trip data as a CSV file and import CSV files. Any files you share or store are handled entirely by you. AtlasDays does not upload, access, or process these files on any server.

5. Analytics & Advertising

AtlasDays collects no analytics, crash reporting, or advertising data. There are no third-party SDKs included in the app.

6. Children's Privacy

AtlasDays does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at atlasdays@pm.me or open an issue on the AtlasDays GitHub repository.
