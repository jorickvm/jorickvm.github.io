Email support

Support is best for one clear operational question, one tracker that looks wrong, one export that seems filtered incorrectly, or one privacy or iCloud sync behavior that does not match the docs.

Email AtlasDays support

For privacy, send only the minimum personal data needed to explain the issue. Avoid passport scans, full document uploads, or anything more sensitive than necessary.

Include these details

  • what you expected and what AtlasDays showed instead
  • the exact countries and dates involved, and whether the trips are Exact Dates, Year, Unknown, ongoing, Transit, duplicate, overlapping, or imported from Photos or CSV
  • the selected tracker window, dashboard period, import date range, or export period if the issue depends on filtered output
  • whether Photos access, Auto-Detect Trips, notification permission, or iCloud sync were enabled, and whether the app had been force-quit if the issue involved background behavior
  • screenshots of the relevant tracker, timeline entries, import preview, export screen, or permissions state
  • your iPhone model, iOS version, and AtlasDays app version if it looks like a bug
