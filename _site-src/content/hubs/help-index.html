Get started

How do I get started with AtlasDays?

Choose your home-country setup, add the trips that affect today’s counts, then create your first tracker.

How do I use the Timeline and Calendar?

Switch views, find trips and photos, review Auto-Detect suggestions, show US stays, and resolve overlaps.

Why do my Dashboard or Map totals look wrong?

Check the selected period, your home country, ongoing trips, Transit days, and US state records first.

Trips and imports

Which trip mode should I use?

Use Exact Dates when you know them; Year, Unknown, Transit, and Open-ended each have different effects on your record.

How does Photo Import work?

AtlasDays scans location metadata on your device, suggests trips, and lets you review duplicates and overlaps before saving.

How do I import a CSV?

Format the required columns, include US State rows when needed, and review row errors and overlap warnings in the preview.

How do I import from Flighty?

Export your Flighty history as CSV, then review how AtlasDays turns flights into stays, transit days, and overnight trips.

Trackers and sharing

Why does a tracker total look wrong?

Review the tracker’s places, time window, count mode, trip precision, and Smart Alert settings.

How do I add a Home Screen widget?

Add a World Map or Tracker widget from iOS, then choose its tracker, size, and forecast display.

How do I export my travel history?

Choose CSV or PDF and the right date range, then check what the export includes before sharing it.
