What's New in AtlasDays

A summary of new features and improvements in each release.

Version 0.6.1 — March 12, 2026

Changed

  • Country flag and name stay centered on the map detail sheet regardless of how many trips are logged
  • Transit trips now appear inline with regular trips in the country detail sheet, sorted newest first
  • Notes field in Add/Edit Trip starts as a single line and grows as you type
  • Trip cards in the country detail sheet are now tappable across the full card
  • Close button on the full-screen map is larger with a styled background and press animation

Fixed

  • Trips added from the country detail sheet now appear immediately without reopening the sheet
  • Transit badge was invisible in dark mode

Version 0.6.0 — March 11, 2026

New

  • Settings tab — a dedicated screen for all your preferences, replacing the World Map tab
  • Toggle auto-detect country changes and push notifications directly from Settings
  • Choose your home country in Settings (with a "No fixed residence" option)
  • iCloud Sync toggle — turn iCloud backup on or off from Settings
  • Set a default trip mode (Exact or Approximate) for new trips
  • Theme picker — switch between System, Light, and Dark appearance
  • CSV export, import, and template download from Settings
  • Delete All Trips & Trackers option with a safety confirmation
  • Links to Privacy Policy, Send Feedback, and What's New in Settings
  • Re-run onboarding at any time from Settings
  • Density toggle in the Trips toolbar — switch between comfortable and compact layout

Changed

  • The world map is now accessible by tapping the minimap on the Summary tab
  • Menus on the Trips and Summary tabs have been simplified — most options moved to Settings
  • Country picker rows are now fully tappable across their entire width
  • The "365 days" rolling window label was corrected (previously said 360 days)

Fixed

  • Notification toggle no longer gets stuck after granting permission in iOS Settings
  • Home country selection now saves reliably

Version 0.5.1 — March 11, 2026

New

  • Re-run onboarding from the Summary tab menu
  • "No fixed residence" option at the top of the home country picker during onboarding
  • Optional residency tracker when selecting a home country during onboarding
  • "Log your first trip" page now always appears at the end of onboarding

Changed

  • Onboarding home country page redesigned — simpler country selection with an optional residency tracker
  • "I'm a nomad" checkbox replaced by a "No fixed residence" option in the country picker

Fixed

  • Location detection no longer suggests your home country right after onboarding

Version 0.5.0 — March 9, 2026

New

  • Country detail sheet — tap any country on the world map to see your trip history there, including trip count and total days spent
  • Add Trip button directly from the country detail sheet
  • Unvisited countries show an info sheet with an option to add a trip
  • Full-screen map by tapping the minimap on the Summary tab
  • Map color style toggle — choose between blue or greyscale themes for visited countries

Changed

  • Tapping a country on the map now opens a detail sheet instead of briefly showing the country name
  • Transit countries (airside) are no longer colored on the map

Version 0.4.0 — March 8, 2026

New

  • New tracker counting mode: Per Stay (alongside Rolling and Calendar Year)
  • Optional "Tracking starts on" date to ignore days before a chosen date
  • Redesigned day selection with preset chips and press-and-hold fast adjustment
  • Country flags in the country picker
  • Schengen-first preset flow with a "Show more presets" option
  • Splash screen with branded logo animation on startup

Changed

  • Tracker editor reorganized into clearer sections
  • Window presets refined to common visa windows
  • App performance improvements — faster startup and smoother scrolling

Version 0.3.0 — March 5, 2026

New

  • iCloud sync — trip data is automatically backed up to your personal iCloud
  • Undo banner when deleting a trip — tap within 4 seconds to restore it
  • "Date unknown" toggle in Approximate mode for trips where you don't remember the exact dates
  • Delete All Trips option with confirmation
  • Delete Trip button in the trip edit form

Changed

  • Date precision simplified from three options to two: Exact and Approximate
  • Trips with a future end date now show as Ongoing with days-so-far
  • Open-ended trips show "Now" instead of "Today"
  • Exact date trips can now have future dates — useful for pre-adding planned trips
  • Improved CSV format with clearer column order and flexible month input (number, abbreviation, or full name)

Fixed

  • Country suggestions no longer appear incorrectly after confirming a trip
  • Suggestion banner timing issues resolved

Version 0.2.0 — February 28, 2026

New

  • Trip list with sections by year, Ongoing, and No Date
  • Exact date trips with full visa day calculation
  • Approximate trips (month/year or year-only) for historical records
  • Airside transit flag for layovers that count as 0 days
  • Auto-detection — the app suggests starting or ending a trip when you enter a new country
  • In-app suggestion banner and notification when a new country is detected
  • Summary tab with visa day totals, custom date ranges, and rolling window presets
  • Visa rule trackers (e.g. Schengen 90/180) with progress bar and days remaining
  • Interactive world map showing visited and transit countries
  • CSV export and import
  • Appearance toggle (system / light / dark)
  • Flag icons for all countries

Version 0.1.0 — December 22, 2025

  • Initial release — basic trip logging with country, dates, and notes
  • Early summary view with year picker and Schengen day totals
