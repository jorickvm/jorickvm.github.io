FAQ

Practical questions before you trust it

The product has more depth than the first screenshot shows, so the important questions are mostly about control, privacy, and flexibility.

Can it track Schengen 90/180 and other limits?

Yes. AtlasDays supports rolling windows, per-stay limits, calendar-year counting, and custom trackers.

Can I add older trips with incomplete dates?

Yes. AtlasDays supports exact trips, approximate month and year ranges, year-only entries, and unknown-date visits.

Does it store my precise location?

No. Country detection is optional and designed around local-only country suggestions, not location surveillance.

Can I import travel history I already have?

Yes. You can import CSV, export your data, and use the built-in template and import guidance to clean up existing records.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Your trips, trackers, and map stay available on-device without an internet connection.

Is this legal or tax advice?

No. AtlasDays helps you keep records and count days. Interpretation of immigration or tax rules is still your responsibility.

How much will AtlasDays cost?

Pricing details will be announced closer to launch.

Is there an Android version?

No Android version is planned yet. AtlasDays is currently focused on iPhone and iPad.
