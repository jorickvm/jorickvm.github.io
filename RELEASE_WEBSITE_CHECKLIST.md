# AtlasDays Release → Website Checklist

For every AtlasDays app release, decide whether each item is **updated**, **verified unchanged**, or **not applicable**.

- Help copy for changed screens, settings, imports, exports, trackers, privacy, sync, Pro, and widgets.
- Deterministic screenshots and `_site-src/data/screenshots.json` app version/capture metadata.
- Homepage claims, plan wording, FAQs, and product proof.
- Changelog entry and App Store destination.
- Privacy page for storage, permissions, location, Photos, analytics, or sync changes.
- Terms for pricing, subscriptions, eligibility, or legal-boundary changes.
- Search index, social cards, sitemap, and generated HTML.
- Relevant Learn links where app workflows are mentioned; do not change fact-verification dates for product-only edits.
- Strict audit, generated-output checks, screenshot/image checks, and representative keyboard/VoiceOver smoke tests.
