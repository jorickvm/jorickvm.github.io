What's New in AtlasDays

New features and improvements in each release.

Version 0.9.4 — April 1, 2026

New

  • Year filter on Timeline — quickly filter your trips by year with a new pill selector, plus collapse/expand all sections
  • PDF transit column — optionally include a transit column in your PDF travel reports

Improved

  • Simpler onboarding — a more streamlined 4-page setup flow
  • Better Settings — rate the app, manage your subscription, and a cleaner layout for Pro subscribers
  • Smarter location detection — more reliable permission handling and background upgrade prompts
  • Stricter CSV import — catches invalid transit values and incomplete date fields before importing

Fixed

  • Day counts could be off by one in certain timezones around midnight
  • Tracker alerts could show negative "days to go" values
  • Dashboard didn't update when editing approximate trip years
  • Deleting a tracker could leave behind orphaned notifications

Version 0.9.3 — March 31, 2026

New

  • Smart Alerts — get notified at custom day thresholds when you have an active trip in a tracked country
  • Dedicated auto-detect settings — manage location-based trip detection from a dedicated settings screen

Improved

  • Redesigned tracker editor — renamed "Per Stay" to "Per Entry", optional custom name, and a more compact layout
  • Better CSV import guidance — clearer help text with step-by-step instructions
  • Various layout and polish improvements

Fixed

  • Re-running onboarding could bypass the free-tier tracker limit

Version 0.9.2 — March 29, 2026

New

  • Night counting — count nights (midnight crossings) instead of days in PDF reports and individual trackers

Improved

  • Redesigned export screen — side-by-side CSV and PDF buttons, trip preview, and cleaner options
  • Cleaner PDF reports — streamlined first page with a tighter layout
  • Various layout and polish improvements

Fixed

  • Date pickers could shift your selection by one day depending on your timezone
  • Pro features now unlock instantly after purchase

Version 0.9.1 — March 28, 2026

New

  • Personalize — choose your accent color and app icon to make AtlasDays yours
  • Country group presets — quickly select all Schengen, EU, or continent countries when setting up a tracker

Improved

  • Tracker management — swipe to delete, tap to edit, and a cleaner context menu
  • Smoother Pro upgrade flow — configure your tracker fully before being prompted to upgrade
  • Various layout and polish improvements

Version 0.9.0 — March 27, 2026

New

  • PDF export — generate a professional travel report with country flags, trip tables, country summary, and tracker progress bars
  • Customizable reports — add your traveler details, group trips by country, include tracker summaries, and toggle branding
  • AtlasDays Pro — unlock premium features like PDF export

Improved

  • Redesigned export — choose your time period and format (CSV or PDF) from a single screen
  • Smarter location suggestions — multiple suggestions can queue up, with better date range display
  • Various layout and polish improvements

Fixed

  • Date pickers could shift your selection by one day depending on your timezone

Version 0.8.3 — March 25, 2026

New

  • Simplified date entry — past trips now use a year picker instead of month+year; year ranges are expanded into individual trips automatically
  • Set home country from the dashboard — tap the banner or long-press the progress bar in Days Abroad to pick your home country directly
  • VoiceOver support — screen reader labels across all views

Improved

  • Smarter tracker colors — the progress bar adapts to your tracker size, turning orange at 7 days remaining and red at 3
  • Schengen preset updated — now includes Monaco, Vatican City, and San Marino (32 countries)
  • Simpler CSV format — 5-column layout with smart date detection; old exports still import fine
  • Various layout and polish improvements

Version 0.8.2 — March 24, 2026

New

  • Swipe to delete trips — swipe left on any trip to quickly remove it, with undo support
  • Overlap detection — the app detects overlapping trips and helps you resolve them

Improved

  • Tidier Timeline toolbar — sort, search, and more are now grouped in a single menu
  • Better share cards — larger logo, theme-aware design, and a cleaner layout
  • Various layout and polish improvements

Version 0.8.1 — March 22, 2026

New

  • Tracker presets — choose from Residence, Schengen, Visa Limit, or Travel Goal when adding a tracker, with all settings pre-filled
  • Dashboard highlight cards — each stat sheet now opens with a visual summary: home vs. abroad split, world progress ring, favorite destination, or continent grid
  • Collapsible date picker — the dashboard period selector now collapses to save space

Improved

  • Redesigned tracker cards — larger flags, gradient progress bars, and a cleaner layout showing your day count at a glance
  • Smoother tracker creation — preset picker and edit form are now a single step with back navigation
  • Various layout and polish improvements

Fixed

  • Dashboard animations could occasionally fail to play on launch

Improved
