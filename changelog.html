App Store launch

v1.0

April 2, 2026

First public release of AtlasDays - a private travel history, day counter, and reporting tool for iPhone.

Included in this release

  • Travel timeline - log exact trips, older approximate history, and transit records in one continuous timeline.
  • Visa and residency trackers - Schengen 90/180, rolling windows, per-entry limits, fiscal-year periods, and custom day thresholds.
  • Dashboard overview - world map, country totals, days abroad, and yearly breakdowns.
  • Threshold alerts - notifications as you approach any visa or residency day limit.
  • CSV and PDF export - export your records or generate printable travel reports.
  • On-device and private - no account required, with optional iCloud sync.
