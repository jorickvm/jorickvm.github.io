What you can do with AtlasDays

  • Keep your travel history in one place — log exact trips, older approximate history, and transit records in the same timeline
  • Track visa and residency limits — monitor Schengen 90/180, rolling windows, per-stay limits, and custom thresholds from the same trip record
  • Review your travel at a glance — see your map, country totals, yearly patterns, and days abroad from the dashboard
  • Get alerts before important thresholds — see used days, remaining days, and warning points before you run out of time
  • Export and report your records — export CSV files and generate PDF travel reports when you need a portable record
  • Keep it private — AtlasDays works on-device with no account required, with optional iCloud sync
