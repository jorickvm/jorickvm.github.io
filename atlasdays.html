Smart Visa Trackers

Set up rules like Schengen 90/180 with rolling windows, calendar year, or per-stay counting. Track multiple countries at once with presets for Schengen, MERCOSUR, CARICOM, GCC, and more.

📍

Auto-Detection

Cross a border and AtlasDays notices. Low-power location monitoring detects country changes and suggests logging a trip — even in the background.

🗺️

Interactive World Map

See every country you've visited on a pannable, zoomable map. Tap any country for your trip history, total days spent, and quick-add a new trip.

Tax Residency Tracking

Built-in 183-day rolling tracker for tax residency rules. Set a "stay under" limit or a "reach target" goal — AtlasDays counts both ways.

Airside Transit

Mark trips as airport transit so they show on your map but don't count toward visa days. Because a layover isn't a visit.

Flexible Dates

Know the exact dates? Great. Only remember the month? That works too. AtlasDays supports exact, month/year, and year-only entries for incomplete travel history.

iCloud Sync

Your trips sync seamlessly across all your Apple devices through your personal iCloud. No account to create, no servers involved.
