FAQ

Practical questions people ask before trusting a travel-day tracker.

Does AtlasDays store my precise location?

No. The app is positioned around travel-day tracking, with privacy as a core design goal. Your travel history is meant to stay yours.

Can it track Schengen 90/180?

Yes. AtlasDays is built for rolling-window visa counting and similar country or region-based limits.

Can I add older trips with incomplete dates?

Yes. AtlasDays is designed to be more realistic than tools that only work when every historic date is perfectly known.

Is this legal or tax advice?

No. AtlasDays helps you keep records and count days. The underlying legal interpretation remains your responsibility.

Can I import past travel history?

Yes. AtlasDays supports bringing in older travel data so you do not have to rebuild everything manually.

Does it sync anywhere?

It can use your own iCloud for sync. The core positioning is private travel tracking without mandatory accounts.
